## Wrong Slides Bug — Post-Mortem

*What happened:* Draft #19 (Spotify AI DJ LinkedIn post) published with Sony Q1 earnings carousel slides attached. Completely wrong topic.

*Root cause:* The `post-001-v3` Sony slides (from May 8 working session) were the only slides ever uploaded to Postiz. Their upload URLs got written to draft #19's `media_urls` column before `approve.js` ran. When `watch-reactions` detected 👍 at 4:11 AM, `approve.js` blindly attached the pre-existing media URLs to the Spotify copy.

*The bugs:*
1. `approve.js` doesn't validate that slide content matches post topic
2. Stale Postiz URLs persisted and got reused on an unrelated draft
3. LinkedIn text posts shouldn't have carousel slides unless explicitly generated for them

*Fixes needed:* Content-match validation before publish, clear `media_urls` on non-carousel drafts, wipe stale URLs.
