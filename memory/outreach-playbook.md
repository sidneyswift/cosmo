# LinkedIn Outreach Playbook — Advisory Revenue Sprint

## Goal: Book 4+ advisory sessions/month ($10k+ MRR from advisory alone)

Sid has 2.6k LinkedIn connections and credibility that most music-AI founders would kill for. This is the fastest path to revenue.

## Target Segments (in priority order)

1. **Label GMs / Heads of Marketing** (15-50 artist rosters) — biggest pain, biggest budgets
2. **Catalog owners / investment funds** — dormant revenue they're losing daily
3. **Artist managers with 3+ clients** — time-starved, need leverage
4. **Distributors** — looking for value-adds for their clients

## DM Templates

### Template 1: The Warm Reconnect (for existing connections)
```
Hey [Name] — been a while. I've been heads-down building AI agents that handle music marketing ops (content, audience research, release campaigns).

Curious — how's your team handling marketing workload right now? I've been advising a few labels on the transition and the results have been wild.
```

### Template 2: The Specific Value (for label GMs)
```
Hey [Name] — saw [their label] just signed [artist/had a release]. Nice move.

Random question: how many people on your marketing team right now? I've been helping labels cut marketing costs 50-70% with AI agents while actually increasing output. Happy to share what's working if useful.
```

### Template 3: The Catalog Hook (for catalog owners)
```
Hey [Name] — quick thought on your catalog. Most catalogs lose 15-20% streaming revenue year-over-year without active marketing. AI agents can reverse that at a fraction of what a human team would cost.

I just published a breakdown on this: [link to catalog blog post]. Worth a read if your catalog revenue has been trending down.
```

### Template 4: The Social Proof (after they engage)
```
Yeah, the math is pretty compelling. One label I advise went from 4 marketing coordinators to 1 + AI agents — saved $180k/year and actually increased content output.

I do 90-minute strategy sessions where I audit your workflows and build a specific AI roadmap. $2,500 and it typically identifies $100k+ in annual savings. Want me to send details?
```

## Outreach Cadence
- **10 DMs per day** (LinkedIn limits ~100/week for free accounts)
- **Monday-Thursday** — best response rates
- **Follow up once** after 5 days if no response
- **Track in Attio** — tag contacts with "outreach-advisory" pipeline stage

## Amplification
- Post 2-3x/week on LinkedIn about AI + music ops (drives inbound)
- Share blog posts as LinkedIn articles/posts
- Comment on target prospects' posts before DMing (warms them up)

## Revenue Projection
- 10 DMs/day × 4 days/week = 40 DMs/week
- 15-20% response rate = 6-8 conversations/week
- 10% conversion to call = 2-3 calls/month
- 50% close rate at $2,500 = $2,500-3,750/month from outreach alone
- Plus inbound from blog/audit funnel
