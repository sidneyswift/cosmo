- Skill file: `apps/web/agent/skills/lesson-planner/SKILL.md`

- Fills the gap identified from Carolyng's Trophies drops: Homa had worksheets (layer 3) and curriculum planning (layer 1), but was missing the orchestration layer (layer 2) that tells parents what to do each day
- Product insight: "the difference between handing someone a stack of recipes and handing them a meal plan with a grocery list"

**Curriculum ingestion JSON:**
- `cur-2026-07-18-harcourt-trophies-2nd.json` — full four-layer architecture analysis of the Trophies system
- Mapped 12 curriculum topics (0 mapped to existing taxonomy, 8 unmapped, 2 no-subject, 2 partial)
- 8 decisions needed (new skill, 5 new recipes, 2 taxonomy additions)
- Key architectural insight: four-layer model (lesson planner → daily instruction → practice materials → assessment) + cross-curricular centers as lateral extension

**Three HTML demo files:**
1. `weekly-planner-demo.html` — landscape, 5 color-coded day columns × 9 skill rows, arc ribbon, materials footer
2. `daily-guide-demo.html` — portrait, 7 activity blocks with numbered teaching steps, differentiation boxes (🚀/🌱), time estimates
