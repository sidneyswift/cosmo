### 3. LinkedIn Baseline Analysis
- Pulled 81 unique posts via Apify (harvestapi/linkedin-profile-posts, $0.002/post)
- Stored in content.db → linkedin_posts table
- Full analysis saved to data/linkedin-analysis.md
- Key findings:
  - Image posts avg 22.7 engagement vs text-only 8.9 (2.5x)

  - Winning formula: specific personal experience + visual + 50-150 words + tension hook
