## Key Findings

### The Algorithm Shift
- Organic reach down **50% YoY**, follower growth down **59%** (Richard van der Blom algorithm insights report)
- March 2026 "Authenticity Update" - NLP now detects and penalizes engagement bait phrases. "Comment YES for the PDF" = shadowban
- New **"Depth Score"** replaced likes as primary ranking signal. Dwell time is now #1
- 3-phase evaluation: initial 2-5% of network sees post → first 60-90 min engagement velocity determines everything → phase 3 = viral distribution (40-60x impressions vs phase 1)
- External links get **60% reach penalty**. Zero-click content is the new standard
- LinkedIn shifted from recency to relevance in late 2025 - older posts resurfacing more
- Saves and Sends added as analytics signals (what LinkedIn actually values now)

### What Formats Win at TOF
1. **Multi-image posts (3-4 photos)** - 6.60% engagement rate, best overall performer. Personal photos beat stock images
2. **Carousels/PDF documents** - 596% more engagement than text-only, 4x more reach than last year. 2.3x higher reach than text, 4.1x vs external links. Sweet spot: 6-10 slides, under 12 words per slide, hook on slide 1
3. **Polls** - 206% more reach than average. NOT anonymous (you can see who voted what - prospecting gold)
4. **Text-only** - Still works when writing is strong. No image > bad image
5. **Video** - Reach crashed 35%, BUT builds trust better than anything. 30-90 seconds, always captions (85% watch silent), native upload only (YouTube links crushed)
6. **Single images** - DEAD. 30% worse than plain text now. "Always add an image" advice from 2024 is actively hurting reach

### TOF Content Types That Work
- **Contrarian takes / bold predictions** (the 20% TOF slice of the B2B funnel)
- **Painful lessons / failure stories** - often outperform wins. Vulnerability creates connection but must be authentic
- **Unpopular opinions backed by evidence** - not just hot air
- **Behind-the-scenes / real process** - not polished versions
- **Perspective shifts** - "what I used to believe vs what I know now"
- **"How I [did X]"** outperforms **"How to [do X]"** every time - readers want stories not advice

### Hook Rules (The First 3 Lines)
- LinkedIn shows only 3 lines in feed preview. ~210 chars desktop, ~140 mobile (57%+ is mobile)
- **Keep hooks under 8 words**
- Use numbers - they grab attention
- Curiosity gap psychology: "I tried X for 30 days..."
- "How I" > "How to" always

### Posting Cadence
- **2-3x/week with high quality beats daily with mixed quality**
- Low-engagement posts create negative momentum affecting subsequent posts ("engagement momentum effect")
- One client: reduced 5x/week to 2x/week → 127% more impressions, engagement rate 1.8% → 4.3%
- Best times: Tue-Thu 7-9 AM and 12-1 PM in audience timezone. BUT secondary windows (Mon/Fri mornings) have less competition

### Personal vs Company Pages
- **Founder profiles outperform company pages by 315% in engagement and 270% in conversions**
- Company pages reach only **1.6% of followers**
- Company page content = ~1-2% of overall LinkedIn feed
- Personal brand IS the top of funnel strategy

### What Gets Penalized
- Engagement pods / fake engagement
- Automation comments (LinkedIn now explicitly throttles visibility)
- Engagement bait phrases detected by NLP
- External links in main caption (-60% reach)
- Generic "Great post!" replies
- Fast engagement spikes with no depth
- Low-quality content that creates negative momentum

### The New Engagement Hierarchy
**Gaining weight:** Saves → Sends (DM shares) → Thoughtful comments → Time spent reading → Engagement beyond first hour
**Losing weight:** Likes alone → Automation comments → Pod engagement → Fast spikes

### The Zero-Click Content Model
Full value must be delivered natively within LinkedIn. If you must share a link, put it in comments or profile featured section. The algorithm wants LinkedIn to be a destination, not a portal.

## Tactical Playbook for Sid/Recoup TOF

Given this research, for a founder-led B2B SaaS LinkedIn TOF strategy:
1. **Lead with carousels (6-10 slides)** with original frameworks/data - highest reach format
2. **20% of posts should be contrarian takes / bold predictions** about AI in music industry
3. **Personal profile > company page** by a factor of 3x
4. **"How I" stories** about building Recoup, AI decisions, failures, pivots
5. **2-3 posts/week max**, never post just to post
6. **Zero external links** in post body - full value in-feed
7. **Hook under 8 words**, mobile-first
8. **Multi-image with real photos** when applicable (team, product, behind-scenes)
