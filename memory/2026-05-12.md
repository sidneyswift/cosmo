## Editorial OS v9 Style Guide Integration (~10:30 PM)

### Sid's Direction
Sid shared the "Recoup Editorial Operating System v9" style guide — a much more refined visual direction for content media. Key shift: from anime art to *premium editorial poster aesthetic*. "Infrastructure frames emotion."

### Key Visual Rules (Editorial OS v9)
- **Not anime** — editorial poster energy. References: Apple campaigns, A24 poster systems, Kinfolk magazine, Swiss editorial design, Arc Browser, Linear, Monocle
- **45%+ negative space** — whitespace is part of the design
- **Max 3 panels, 1 primary idea** — simple at phone-screen size
- **Modular editorial framing** with thin architectural borders
