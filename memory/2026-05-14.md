# 2026-05-14

## Nightly Build: Free AI Readiness Audit at /audit (1:00 AM)

### What I Built
- **Interactive AI audit page** at `/audit` — 7-question quiz that scores music companies on AI readiness
- `AuditForm` client component with 3 steps: questions → contact capture → personalized results
- Scoring algorithm maps answers to 3 tiers (Low/Mid/High) with tailored CTAs
- Contact info (name, email, company) + audit answers → Attio CRM via existing `/api/subscribe`
- Progress bar, back navigation, clean dark UI matching site design
- Copy file: `lib/copy/audit.ts` — centralized questions, scoring tiers, and form labels
- Committed to marketing submodule (commit 32ec495)

### Why This Matters
- **The funnel had a $2,500 gap.** The jump from free playbook → $2,500 advisory session is too big. The audit creates a middle step: engage prospects with a personalized assessment, capture their info, and route them to the right offer based on their actual situation.
- **Self-qualifying leads.** By the time someone finishes the quiz, we know their role, roster size, budget, bottlenecks, and AI maturity. That's a sales call's worth of qualification — for free.
- **Every answer is data.** Audit responses stored in Attio tell Sid exactly who's interested and what they need. A label owner with 50+ artists spending $15k/mo on marketing ops who scores "Ready to Scale" is a $10k advisory lead.
- **Shareable on LinkedIn.** "I built a 2-minute quiz that tells you if your label is ready for AI. Take it:" → link. Interactive content gets 2-5x more engagement than static posts.

### Scoring Logic
- Roster size, marketing hours, content volume, budget → higher = more points
- AI usage → inversely scored (less current usage = more upside to sell)
- Low (0-4): → playbook + $2,500 strategy session
- Mid (5-9): → $10,000 AI Transformation + free trial
- High (10+): → Partner tier + pricing page
