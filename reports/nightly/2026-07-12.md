## Phase 3: What I Built

### AI Readiness Assessment Tool (`consulting-os/library/tools/ai-readiness-assessment.html`)

**Single-file, self-contained HTML** — 30KB, no dependencies, works offline, opens in any browser.

**5 assessment categories, 10 questions:**
1. Content & Marketing (2 questions) — social content creation, press materials
2. Catalog & Data (2 questions) — streaming analytics, metadata/rights/royalties
3. Fan Engagement (2 questions) — fan communication, superfan identification
4. Operations (2 questions) — A&R research, internal reporting
5. Team & Workflow (2 questions) — manual task burden, AI adoption maturity

**4-tier scoring system:**
- 🔴 AI Unaware (0-25%) — "You're leaving 20+ hours/week on the table"
- 🟡 AI Curious (26-50%) — "You've started but there's massive untapped potential"
- 🟢 AI Adopter (51-75%) — "Good foundation, strategic gaps remain"
- 🚀 AI Native (76-100%) — "You're ahead — let's optimize"

**Results page includes:**
- Animated score ring with count-up animation
- Category breakdown with color-coded progress bars
- Top 3 personalized recommendations (based on lowest-scoring categories)
- ROI calculator (estimated hours/week recoverable, monthly & annual savings)
- CTA → Book Free Strategy Call (Calendly link)

**Lead capture gate:** Name, email, company fields before full results (with skip option). Console-logs the data — ready to wire to any backend.

**Design:** Dark mode (#0a0a0a), Recoup brand, smooth animations, fully mobile responsive. Professional enough for a VP at a major label.
