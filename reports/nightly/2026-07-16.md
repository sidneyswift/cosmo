### 2. Interactive Artist Intelligence Demo — Live at recoupable.dev/demo/artist-intelligence.html

**Built from scratch** — 42KB self-contained HTML, zero dependencies. Uses realistic data for Joy Crookes (the example artist from the research plugin docs).

**6 sections, each demonstrating a research plugin capability:**

1. **Streaming Analytics** — 14-platform metrics with sparkline charts, monthly listener trends, platform-by-platform breakdown with animated bar charts. Shows: 3M monthly listeners, 12.4% MoM growth, cross-platform reach.

2. **TikTok Per-Song Velocity** (tagged #1 Customer Ask) — per-track table with creation counts, 7-day velocity, Spotify stream conversion ratios. Shows "When You Were Mine" in active viral growth (+42% velocity) with 2.7x conversion. This is the exact data format customers ask for most.

3. **Audience Intelligence** — demographics (age/gender split) + geographic breakdown with emerging market detection. Shows Japan at +340% growth as an emerging opportunity. Two-column responsive layout.

4. **Competitive Landscape** — similar artist cards with similarity scores, plus a playlist gap analysis table showing editorial playlists that peers are on but the artist isn't. Shows "Are & Be" (5.2M followers) as the #1 pitch target.

5. **Growth Recommendations** — AI-generated action plan with P0/P1 priorities, specific tactics, and estimated impact. Each recommendation is actionable (not vague) with dollar/stream projections.

6. **What Powers This** — maps each section back to a specific Recoup research plugin skill (`/recoup-research`, TikTok per-song, playlist intelligence, competitive analysis, trend detection, weekly briefs).

**Design:** Recoup dark mode (#0a0a0a), shadow-as-border technique, animated bars and sparklines on scroll, fully responsive. Professional enough to send to a VP at a major label.

**Every section has an "insight" callout** with a specific, actionable recommendation — not just data, but what to DO with the data. This is the Recoup value prop in action: intelligence + recommendations, not just metrics.
