## Phase 3: What I Built

### Interactive Enterprise Value Assessment

**File:** `marketing/public/demo/enterprise-value-assessment.html` (44KB, self-contained, zero dependencies)

**3-Step Interactive Flow:**

**Step 1 — Company Profile:**
- Company type selector: Record Label, Distributor, Publisher, Management, Catalog Acquirer, Other
- Team size slider (3-200)
- Artists managed slider (5-500)
- Monthly releases slider (1-100)

**Step 2 — Current Operations:**
- Multi-select operations checklist: Content creation, Performance reporting, Artist research & analytics, Social media management, Deal analysis & ingestion, Catalog management
- Weekly hours slider (whole team, 5-200h)
- Monthly agency/contractor spend input
- Average hourly cost slider ($20-$150)

**Step 3 — Personalized Results:**

1. **KPI Strip** — Four headline metrics:
   - Monthly Net Savings (green, dollar amount)
   - Hours Reclaimed per month (accent)
   - Content Velocity multiplier (amber)
   - Projected ROI (blue)

2. **Monthly Cost Comparison Chart** — Animated bar chart showing:
   - Today's spend (red: agency + staff time on manual work)
   - Recoup cost (green)
   - Net savings (indigo)

3. **Savings Breakdown** — Line-by-line source of savings with color-coded dots:
   - Agency/contractor replacement (85% for content, 50% for other)
   - Content creation automation (85% hours reclaimed)
   - Automated performance reporting (95% hours reclaimed)
   - Artist research & analytics (70%)
   - Social media management (60%)
   - Deal analysis & ingestion (50%)
   - Catalog management (40%)
   - Minus: Recoup plan cost
   - Equals: Net monthly savings

4. **Tabbed Deep-Dive:**
   - **📊 Evidence** — Three verified case study cards with real metrics:
     - Rostrum: $5K agency replaced, 30× faster, 30 days content/session
     - Fat Beats: $10K creative director replaced, 72h vs 3 weeks
     - Parlophone: 40+ hours/month automated, 5→0 staff on reports
   - **⚔️ Why Not Alternatives** — Feature comparison table: Recoup vs Content Agencies vs In-House Team vs General AI (ChatGPT). 8 capability rows including cost at prospect's scale.
   - **🚀 Recommendation** — Personalized plan card with recommended tier (Standard $5K / Premium $10K / Enterprise $15K), feature list, and ROI badge

5. **Time to Value** — 3-phase visual: Week 1 → Month 1 → Month 3 milestones

6. **Downloadable Summary** — "Download PDF Summary" button generates a text report

**Pricing Logic:**
- Team ≤10 → Standard ($5K/mo, 25 seats)
- Team 11-50 → Premium ($10K/mo, 50 seats)
- Team 51+ → Enterprise ($15K/mo, unlimited)

**Automation Rates** (derived from case study evidence):
- Content: 85% (Rostrum replaced entire agency)
- Reporting: 95% (Parlophone fully automated)
- Research: 70% (significant but requires human interpretation)
- Social: 60% (posting automated, strategy human)
- Deals: 50% (analysis automated, decisions human)
- Catalog: 40% (metadata partially automated)

**Design:** Recoup dark mode (#0a0a0a), shadow-as-border, animated bars and counters, Inter + JetBrains Mono typography, responsive layout. Same quality level as the Artist Intelligence Demo and Creative Campaign Tracker.
