## Phase 3: What I Built

### 1. Legal OS Skill Package — 3 Production-Ready Skills

**Location:** `consulting-os/clients/seeker-music/04-delivery/legal-os/skills/`

**Skill: Contract Review & Redline Analysis** (`contract-review.md`, 135 lines)
- Clause-by-clause analysis of incoming contracts against Seeker standard terms
- Risk scoring per clause: ✅ Aligned, ⚠️ Deviation, 🔴 Risk, ❓ Missing
- Overall contract risk rating (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH/CRITICAL)
- Structured output: executive summary → key terms table → detailed analysis → recommendations
- Every finding cites specific page numbers and clause references
- Framed as "AI drafts, attorney reviews" — respects Dan's near-100% accuracy bar
- Estimated time savings: 4–8 hours → 30 minutes per contract

**Skill: Deal Memo Analysis** (`deal-memo-analysis.md`, 172 lines)
- Normalizes deal room materials into Seeker's IC memo format
- Calculates deal metrics: revenue multiples (T12, T36), revenue trends, concentration analysis, cost per track
- Flags risks at three levels: 🔴 Critical, ⚠️ Material, ℹ️ Note
- Identifies data gaps — generates specific document requests for sellers
- Ties directly to the Q2 SOW deliverable (Deal Ingestion & Analysis Agent)
- Estimated time savings: 2–3 days → 2 hours per deal

**Skill: Rights Chain-of-Title Analysis** (`rights-chain-of-title.md`, 206 lines)
- Maps ownership chains from original creation to current holder for every rights type (master, publishing, sync, mechanical, performance, digital, physical, neighboring)
- Tracks transfer history with document references for every link
- Flags chain breaks, unverified links, and reversion risks (§203/§304 termination rights)
- Produces structured rights map suitable for acquisition due diligence
- Core capability for Seeker's catalog acquisition business ($400M+ PE deal portfolio)
- Estimated time savings: 1–2 days → 1 hour per catalog
