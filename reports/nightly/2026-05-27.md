### 🚨 Bug Found: approve.js Published 8 Legacy Posts Without Thumbnails

**The problem:** When the nightly engine called approve.js to schedule draft #906, approve.js also found 8 legacy "approved" drafts in the Supabase mirror and published ALL of them to Postiz. These 8 legacy posts had no thumbnails (media_urls were stale/non-JSON).

**Output:** "Skipping legacy media_urls (non-JSON, may be stale) → Published (ID: created)" × 8

**Impact:** 8 posts are now on Postiz without thumbnails. This is the same anti-pattern from May 21.

**Root cause:** approve.js queries Supabase for all `status='approved'` drafts, not just the one the nightly engine intended to schedule. No filter for "has valid thumbnail."

**Fix needed:** approve.js should require valid media_urls (JSON with at least one URL) before publishing. Legacy approved posts without thumbnails should be rejected or sent back to the thumbnail queue.
