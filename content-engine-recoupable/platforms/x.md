# X / Twitter — Recoupable

## Platform DNA
- Concise product-focused content
- More polished than Sid's personal X
- Mix of demos, proof posts, feature updates, and industry takes
- Audience: [[labels]], [[artists]], [[builders]]

## Content Rules
- Lead with product value, not company hype
- Always show, don't just tell — screenshots, demos, numbers
- Use [[hooks]] — proof hooks and replacement hooks work best
- Match [[brand-voice]] at its most concise. see [[platform-tone]]
- Links in reply, not main tweet
- No hashtags

## Formats That Work
1. **Product Demo** — short video or screenshot of the platform
   doing something impressive. "Watch our agent create content
   for 10 platforms from one brief:"
2. **Proof Post** — real numbers from real usage. "[Before] →
   [after] using Recoupable"
3. **Feature Announcement** — what shipped, why it matters, who
   it helps. concise
4. **Industry Take** — measured perspective on AI + music news.
   not hot takes — informed analysis

## Posting Strategy
- Frequency: 5x/week
- Best times: 8-9am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm ET
- See [[scheduling]]
