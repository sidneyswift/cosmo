# LinkedIn — Recoupable

## Platform DNA
- Enterprise credibility. this is where label execs see us
- Professional, data-driven, case-study heavy
- Audience: primarily [[labels]] and [[builders]]

## Content Rules
- Lead with business impact. "80% cost reduction" not "cool AI"
- Case studies are king. real numbers, real customers (with permission)
- Industry thought leadership backed by what we're seeing in practice
- Match [[brand-voice]] at its most professional. see [[platform-tone]]
- First line must hook — LinkedIn truncates at ~210 chars
- Links in first comment

## Formats That Work
1. **Case Study** — "How [type of client] went from [before] to
   [after] using our platform"
2. **Industry Analysis** — "Here's what we're seeing in AI + music
   based on powering X accounts across Y labels"
3. **Product Announcement** — what we shipped, the problem it solves,
   who benefits
4. **The Thesis Post** — why AI infrastructure matters for music.
   the co-creation model. deeper positioning content

## Posting Strategy
- Frequency: 3-5x/week
- Best times: 7-8am, 12pm, 5-6pm ET
- See [[scheduling]]
