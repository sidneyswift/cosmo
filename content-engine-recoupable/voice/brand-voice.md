# Brand Voice — Recoupable (Company)

This is the company. Not Sid. Not a personal blog.
A product and platform that backs every claim with proof.

See [[platform-tone]] for per-platform adjustments.

## Core Personality

Confident and proven, not hype-driven. Recoupable builds AI
infrastructure that runs music businesses — and proves it by
running its own label. The voice is authoritative but accessible.
Technical depth when the audience needs it, business impact when
they don't. Never talks down to the industry, never oversells
what the platform can do. Shows, doesn't just tells. Lets the
product and results speak.

## Tone Markers

- Confident but not arrogant — "we built this and here's the proof"
  not "we're the best thing to happen to music"
- Product-focused — always ground in what the platform does,
  not abstractions about AI
- Proof-driven — real numbers, real case studies, real output.
  if you can show a screenshot or demo, show it
- Accessible technical — can explain agent architecture to a
  label exec without dumbing it down or losing them
- Industry-respectful — the music business has been around
  longer than AI. we enhance their expertise, not replace it
- Measured — Recoupable doesn't do hot takes. that's Sid's lane.
  the company is steady, reliable, professional

## Vocabulary

Words Recoupable uses: infrastructure, agents, platform, context,
execute, pipeline, automate, scale, native, integrate, workflow,
co-create

Words Recoupable NEVER uses: disrupt, revolutionary, game-changer,
killing it, crushing it, excited to announce (without substance),
democratize, synergy, paradigm shift

Phrases Recoupable uses:
- "AI infrastructure that runs music businesses"
- "We prove it by running our own"
- "Agents execute, not just answer"
- "Deep artist context"
- "Built for the music business, not adapted for it"

Phrases Recoupable never uses:
- "We're disrupting the music industry"
- "The future of music is here"
- Anything that sounds like it's replacing humans
- Generic AI hype with no product connection

## The Difference From Sid's Voice

| Recoupable (company) | Sid (personal) |
|---------------------|----------------|
| "We" | "I" |
| Professional, polished | Raw, unfiltered |
| Measured takes | Hot takes welcome |
| Product-focused | Journey-focused |
| Case studies | Personal stories |
| Confident | Can be vulnerable |
| Industry-respectful | Contrarian |
| Enterprise-ready | Founder-relatable |

## Formatting Rules
- Standard capitalization (not forced lowercase like Sid's)
- Clean, professional formatting
- Bullet points for scannability
- Numbers and metrics prominent
- Minimal emojis (product-appropriate only)
- Hashtags only on Instagram
- Always include a clear takeaway or CTA
