# Content Types & Formats

## Thread / Multi-Part Post
- Platforms: X (thread), LinkedIn (multi-paragraph), Threads
- When: step-by-step guides, listicles, breakdowns
- Structure: hook → numbered steps → conclusion + CTA

## Carousel / Slide Post
- Platforms: Instagram, LinkedIn (document posts)
- When: visual guides, processes, comparisons, system diagrams
- Structure: slide 1 = hook, slides 2-9 = content, last = CTA

## Short-Form Video
- Platforms: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- When: quick tips, demos, screen recordings, reactions
- Structure: hook (2 sec) → content (30-50 sec) → CTA (5 sec)

## Long-Form Video
- Platforms: YouTube
- When: tutorials, deep dives, system walkthroughs, live builds
- Structure: hook (30 sec) → context (90 sec) → main (6-8 min) → recap

## Long-Form Text
- Platforms: Newsletter, LinkedIn articles
- When: deep analysis, personal stories, comprehensive guides,
  behind-the-scenes
- Length: 1,000-3,000 words

## Short Take
- Platforms: X, Threads, Facebook
- When: hot takes, observations, single insights, quick wins
- Length: under 280 chars (X) or 500 chars (Threads)

## Build Log
- Platforms: X, LinkedIn
- When: shipped something, hit a milestone, learned a lesson
- Structure: what we built → why → what we learned
- Always include a screenshot or demo when possible

## Demo / Screen Recording
- Platforms: TikTok, YouTube, X (video), Instagram Reels
- When: showing the product/system in action
- Keep it raw and real, not overproduced
- Voiceover > face-to-camera for technical demos
