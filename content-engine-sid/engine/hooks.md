# Hook Formulas

Use these to open every post. Match hook type to platform and topic.
See [[platform-tone]] for delivery adjustments.

## The Playbook Hook
"Here's [N] steps to [desirable outcome]:"
- Best on: X, LinkedIn, YouTube titles
- Examples:
  - "here's how I run 10 social accounts without writing a single post:"
  - "here's 5 steps to make your record label AI-native:"

## The Proof Hook
"[Before metric] → [after metric] in [timeframe]"
- Best on: X, LinkedIn
- Examples:
  - "$5k/mo content agency → $0 after building one system"
  - "0 social accounts managed → 10 in 30 days using agents"
  - "manual content for 40 artists → automated in 2 weeks"

## The Contrarian Hook
"You don't need [conventional thing]. You need [this instead]."
- Best on: X, Threads
- Examples:
  - "you don't need a content team. you need an agent with context."
  - "you don't need 15 tools. you need one system that thinks."
  - "your AI content is slop because you're giving it zero context about your artist."

## The Replacement Hook
"I replaced [expensive/complex thing] with [simple thing]"
- Best on: X, TikTok, Instagram slide 1
- Examples:
  - "I replaced a $5k/mo content agency with AI agents and markdown files"
  - "I replaced 8 hours of weekly content creation with a 5-minute prompt"

## The Discovery Hook
"I just found [valuable thing]"
- Best on: X
- Examples:
  - "I just found a way to make AI write differently for 10 platforms from one idea"
  - "I just figured out why 90% of AI-generated music content is garbage"

## The Behind-the-Scenes Hook
"I [do impressive thing] and [surprising method]"
- Best on: X, LinkedIn, YouTube titles
- Examples:
  - "I run 10 social accounts for label clients and don't write a single post"
  - "I take $0 salary and my company is cash-flow positive"

## The Builder Hook
"I just shipped [thing]. here's what I learned:"
- Best on: X, LinkedIn
- Examples:
  - "we just shipped social connectors for our label clients. the hardest part wasn't the tech:"
  - "I just built a content system that thinks about topics 10 different ways. here's how:"

## The Raw Honesty Hook
"I was wrong about [thing]" / "we lost [thing] because [mistake]"
- Best on: LinkedIn, Newsletter
- Examples:
  - "I was wrong about how labels adopt AI tools"
  - "we lost a customer's trust because a third-party service went down"
  - "56% of our churned users got a good first response and never came back"

## Rules
- Test 2-3 hooks per topic before publishing
- Track which types get the most engagement PER PLATFORM
- Update this file weekly based on performance
- Hook for [[x]] is almost never the same as hook for [[linkedin]]
- Always include a specific number or metric when possible
