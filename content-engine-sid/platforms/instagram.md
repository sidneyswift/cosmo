# Instagram

## Platform DNA
- Caption limit: 2,200 characters, but engagement comes from
  the visual (carousel slides or Reels)
- Vibe: visual-first. the image/carousel stops the scroll,
  the caption closes the deal. bold, clean, designed
- Audience: wider and more [[industry]] leaning. visual learners.
  more aspirational

## Content Rules
- Carousels are king. 7-10 slides, one idea per slide
- Slide 1 = the hook. bold claim, large text, something contrarian
  or curiosity-driven. use [[hooks]], discovery and proof hooks
  work best visually
- Match [[brand-voice]] but simpler language. see [[platform-tone]]
- Captions should add context the slides don't cover. don't
  repeat the slides in text form
- Hashtags: 5-10 relevant ones at end of caption. unlike X,
  hashtags actually work on Instagram for discovery
- CTA in the last slide AND caption: "Save this" / "Follow for
  more" / "Share with someone building in music"

## Carousel Design Rules
- Font: bold, sans-serif, high contrast
- One idea per slide. max 30 words per slide
- Consistent color scheme (brand colors)
- Slide 1: big bold text, no more than 8 words
- Last slide: clear CTA with handle

## Posting Strategy
- Frequency: 3-5x/week (2-3 carousels, 1-2 Reels)
- Best times: 11am-1pm, 7-9pm ET
- See [[scheduling]]

## Recoupable-Specific Angles
- System diagrams as carousels (how the agent stack works)
- Before/after comparisons (manual vs agent workflow)
- Behind-the-scenes of building (workspace screenshots, agent output)
- Artist content showcase (Gatsby Grace, with permission)
- Quick tips: "one thing that 10x'd our content pipeline"
