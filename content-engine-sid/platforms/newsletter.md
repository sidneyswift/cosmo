# Newsletter

## Platform DNA
- Email-based. you own the audience (no algorithm)
- 1,000-2,000 words per issue. deep-dive format
- Vibe: direct, personal. like a letter from a smart friend
  who's also building something. most personal of all platforms

## Content Rules
- Subject line = your hook. use [[hooks]] adapted for email.
  "The system that replaced a $5k/mo content agency" not
  "Weekly Newsletter #47"
- Open with a story or personal observation, then transition
  to the tactical stuff
- One core topic per issue. don't try to cover everything
- Match [[brand-voice]] at its most personal. see [[platform-tone]]
- End with ONE clear CTA. reply to the email, check out a
  resource, or try something specific
- Plain text or minimal design. fancy templates = marketing spam.
  plain text = real person

## Recoupable-Specific Angles
- Behind-the-scenes of building Recoupable (the real numbers)
- Deep dives on specific technical decisions
- The founder journey: what's working, what's broken, what's next
- Enterprise lessons: what we learned from label customers
- The label play: Gatsby Grace, AI artists, the thesis
- Exclusive insights not shared on social
