# X / Twitter

## Platform DNA
- Character limit: 280 per tweet, long-form up to 25,000
  (sweet spot is 1,000-2,000 characters)
- Vibe: fast, casual, opinion-driven. lowercase default.
  0.5 seconds to stop the scroll
- Audience here: more technical, more builder-oriented.
  skews toward [[builders]] over [[industry]]

## Content Rules
- Write this platform FIRST in the [[repurpose]] chain.
  X forces brevity which makes everything else easier to expand
- Use [[hooks]] — contrarian hooks and proof hooks perform best
- Match [[brand-voice]] but make it the most casual. see [[platform-tone]]
- Line breaks between every thought. never dense paragraphs
- No hashtags. ever. they look spammy on X
- Emojis sparingly if at all
- Links go in a reply, never in the main tweet (algo penalty)
- Real numbers always. "$5k/mo" not "thousands"

## Formats That Work
1. **The Step-by-Step Thread** — "Here's how I [built/replaced/automated]:"
   followed by numbered steps. most reliable for engagement
2. **The Short Take** — 2-4 lines. bold claim + one-line context +
   punchline. best for contrarian hooks
3. **The Proof Post** — "[Before] → [after] in [timeframe]" followed
   by the breakdown. real numbers are irresistible
4. **The Build Log** — "shipped this today:" with a screenshot or
   demo. building in public content
5. **The Long-Form Tweet** — 1,000-2,000 characters. deep breakdown
   of one idea. 1-2x/week max

## Posting Strategy
- Frequency: 5-7x/week (1-2 posts per day)
- Best times: 8-9am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm ET
- Engage in replies for 30 min after posting
- See [[scheduling]]

## Audience on X
- Primary: [[builders]] — indie hackers, AI engineers, music tech
- They want: actionable content, real numbers, systems, proof
- They don't want: fluff, motivation without substance, "AI will
  change everything" takes without showing HOW
- Address them as "you" — direct, peer energy

## Recoupable-Specific Angles
- Building in public: what we shipped, what broke, what we learned
- AI agent demos: screen recordings of agents executing tasks
- Music industry + AI intersection: nobody else is at this crossroads
- Founder raw takes: $0 salary, self-funded, the real numbers
- Enterprise wins: anonymized or with permission
