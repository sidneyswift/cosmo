# LinkedIn

## Platform DNA
- Sweet spot: 1,300-2,000 characters for regular posts
- Vibe: professional but human. personal narratives win here.
  "I" stories with business lessons attached
- Audience: mix of [[builders]] and [[industry]] — professionals,
  decision-makers, label execs, people with budgets

## Content Rules
- Lead with a personal hook. "I was wrong about..." /
  "6 months ago I..." / "Everyone told me to..."
- First line is EVERYTHING. LinkedIn truncates after ~210 characters.
  hook must be in that first line or nobody clicks "see more"
- Use [[hooks]] — proof hooks and playbook hooks perform best
- Match [[brand-voice]] but more professional. see [[platform-tone]]
- Short paragraphs. one idea per paragraph. white space matters
- No hashtags in the body. max 3 at the very end if any
- Links in the first comment, never in the post body
- The founder journey plays incredibly well here

## Formats That Work
1. **Personal Narrative** — "I [did/learned/failed at something]"
   → lesson → actionable takeaway. LinkedIn's bread and butter
2. **The Listicle Post** — "7 things I learned about [topic]:"
   with numbered items. 1-2 sentences each
3. **The Contrarian Take** — challenge conventional music biz wisdom.
   open with bold statement, follow with reasoning
4. **The Case Study** — real results, real numbers, real process.
   "How we went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]"
5. **Document/Carousel** — PDF slides uploaded as document.
   10-15 slides, one idea per slide

## Posting Strategy
- Frequency: 3-5x/week
- Best times: 7-8am, 12pm, 5-6pm ET
- Comment on 10 relevant posts before publishing (engagement hack)
- See [[scheduling]]

## Audience on LinkedIn
- Mix of [[builders]] and [[industry]]
- They want: professional insights, business lessons, real
  stories, enterprise case studies
- They don't want: overly casual language, memes, hot takes
  without substance
- Higher ticket potential — these people have budgets
- Label execs and music industry decision-makers are here

## Recoupable-Specific Angles
- Founder journey: $0 salary, self-funded, building with agents
- Enterprise co-creation model (no specifics on customers without permission)
- AI infrastructure thesis: why music needs agentic tools
- Hiring philosophy: agents > headcount at this stage
- The adoption gap: product vs manual execution

- Usually SECOND in the [[repurpose]] chain
- Take the X post → add personal narrative, expand to 1,300+ chars
- Add professional framing ("In my experience building for labels...")
- Hook usually needs full rewrite — X hooks don't translate directly
- Can extract slides for [[instagram]] carousels
