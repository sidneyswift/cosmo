# TikTok

## Platform DNA
- Video platform. 15 seconds to 10 minutes, sweet spot 45-90 seconds
- Vibe: raw, unpolished, authentic. overproduced performs WORSE.
  people want real, not perfect
- Audience: youngest. more [[industry]] curious / [[builders]] adjacent.
  discovering AI + music intersection
- Discovery-based algo — don't need followers to go viral

## Content Rules
- Hook in FIRST 2 SECONDS. if you don't grab them, they swipe.
  use [[hooks]], contrarian and discovery hooks adapted for video
- Match [[brand-voice]] at most casual and energetic. see [[platform-tone]]
- Talk TO the camera like talking to a friend
- Screen recordings with voiceover are perfect for showing
  agents in action, the system working, content being generated
- No long intros. no "hey guys." jump straight in
- Captions/text overlays mandatory. most people watch muted

## Formats That Work
1. **The Screen Recording** — show agents doing the thing. record
   screen, add voiceover. 45-60 seconds
2. **The Quick Tip** — one actionable tip in 30 seconds.
   hook → tip → result
3. **The "I Replaced X With Y"** — show the transformation.
   $5k/mo agency → AI agents. manual → automated. compelling
4. **The Demo** — show the actual product/system working in real time
5. **The Reaction** — react to AI-in-music news or bad takes

## Script Template
[HOOK: 2 seconds — text overlay + voiceover]
[CONTEXT: 5 seconds]
[THE HOW: 30-40 seconds — show the system]
[RESULT: 5 seconds]
[CTA: 3 seconds]

## Posting Strategy
- Frequency: 5-7x/week
- Best times: 7-9am, 12-3pm, 7-10pm ET
- Post consistently for 30 days before judging results
- See [[scheduling]]

## Recoupable-Specific Angles
- "Watch this agent create content for 10 platforms in 30 seconds"
- Screen recordings of the system in action
- "I replaced [expensive thing] with [our thing]"
- Quick demos of specific features
- Reacting to music industry AI news
