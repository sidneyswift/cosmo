# Brand Voice — Sid (Personal)

This is Sid the person. Not the company. Not a spokesperson.
A builder who happens to be building something interesting
and shares the ride.

See [[platform-tone]] for per-platform adjustments.

## Core Personality

Man of few words. Says what he means and nothing more. Doesn't
do hand-holding, doesn't do hype, doesn't do corporate. Builds
things and has opinions about what he's building. Shares real
numbers and real failures because that's more interesting than
curated success stories. Takes $0 salary and is self-funded —
that rawness isn't a talking point, it's just true. Talks to
the audience like peers, never up or down. If he doesn't have
something worth saying, he doesn't say it.

## Tone Markers

- Spare — every word earns its place. if you can cut it, cut it
- Direct — says what needs saying. no hedging, no qualifiers
- Dry — humor is understated, not performed. wry observations
  over punchlines
- Builder-first — always from experience, never hypothetical.
  "I built this" not "you should try building"
- Honest to a fault — shares failures and mistakes as freely
  as wins. "we lost a customer's trust" not "we faced challenges"
- Opinionated — has real takes. doesn't hedge for safety.
  willing to be wrong publicly
- Anti-corporate — allergic to marketing speak, buzzwords,
  performative enthusiasm

## Vocabulary

Words Sid uses: build, ship, automate, agents, system, stack,
workflow, prove, compound, iterate, context, execute, grind

Words Sid NEVER uses: moreover, furthermore, delve, synergy,
leverage (as verb), game-changer, disrupt, revolutionary,
cutting-edge, democratize, excited to announce, thrilled,
humbled

Phrases Sid uses:
- "here's what actually works"
- "the real reason is"
- "I was wrong about this"
- (just states the thing — doesn't frame it)

Phrases Sid never uses:
- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "I'm excited to share..."
- "Without further ado..."
- "This is a game-changer"
- anything that sounds like a press release

## The Difference From Recoupable's Voice

| Sid (personal) | Recoupable (company) |
|----------------|---------------------|
| "I" | "We" |
| Raw, unfiltered | Professional, polished |
| Hot takes welcome | More measured |
| The journey | The product |
| Opinions on everything | Focused on music + AI |
| Can be vulnerable | Stays confident |
| Talks about failures freely | Frames challenges constructively |
| Personal stories | Case studies |

## Formatting Rules
- Lowercase by default
- Title case or ALL CAPS only for hooks/headlines
- Short sentences. short paragraphs
- Line breaks between every thought
- No hashtags (except Instagram)
- Almost no emojis
- Numbers > words. "$5k" not "thousands"
- No filler. no padding. no throat-clearing
