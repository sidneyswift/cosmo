## Permanent Rules (data-proven)
- Max 150 words per post (2,177 avg views vs 537 for 150+)
- LinkedIn: 1 post/day, 7 days/week (volume for fast iteration)
- X: post as often as possible (test bed — audience is broader, not music-niche)
- Include 1 question-hook post per week

## Queued Experiments (pick 1-2 per week)
- Personal story hooks ("I built X" / "I learned Y")
- Community prompt posts ("What's your take on X?")
- Specific numbers in every hook
- Image vs text-only A/B test
- Recoup tie-in vs generic AI take
- Time-of-day optimization (morning vs afternoon)
- Thread vs single post on X

## Anti-Patterns (what NOT to do)
- Source-commentary pattern ("X says Y, here's what it means") — engine default, underperforms
- Posts over 150 words — algorithm penalty
- Tuesday/Thursday publishing — lowest reach days
- Generic AI industry takes without personal stake
- Markdown formatting in posts (bold, headers) — LinkedIn doesn't render it
- Starting with "I'm excited to share" or any slop opener
